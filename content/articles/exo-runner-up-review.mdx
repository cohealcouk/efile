# EXO Runner Up E-File Review: Budget-Friendly Professional Option

## Executive Summary

The EXO Runner Up presents an interesting value proposition in the professional e-file market, offering cordless operation at a competitive price point. Our technical analysis reveals a device that balances affordability with essential features for mobile nail technicians and budget-conscious practices.

### Power and Performance
- **Motor Type**: Brushless DC motor

- **Speed Range**: 0-25,000 RPM
- **Battery**: 2000mAh lithium-ion
- **Runtime**: 4 hours continuous
- **Charge Time**: 2 hours

## Performance Analysis

### Torque Delivery
The 4.0 Ncm torque rating places the EXO Runner Up in the mid-range category. Testing showed consistent torque delivery up to 20,000 RPM, with some power droop at maximum speeds.

### Battery Performance
The 2000mAh battery provides approximately 4 hours of continuous use at moderate speeds. Heavy use at maximum RPM reduces runtime to approximately 2.5 hours.

### Speed Control
Digital speed control offers precise adjustment with 100 RPM increments. The single-button interface is intuitive but may take time to master for users accustomed to dial controls.
